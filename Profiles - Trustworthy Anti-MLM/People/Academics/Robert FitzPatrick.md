Robert FitzPatrick is an American advocate against pyramid schemes and Multi-Level Marketing schemes. He wrote the book [[Ponzinomics|Ponzinomics]].

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_L._FitzPatrick

> Robert Fitzpatrick’s interest in pyramid schemes goes back to his own involvement in a pyramid-like scheme in the 1980s. He didn’t lose money, but he witnessed first-hand how you can get sucked into the euphoria of what he calls “delusional behavior.”[^1]
